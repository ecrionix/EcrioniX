Real Examples

Google TPU v4 Pod (8 chips): - Mesh topology (2Г—2Г—2 cube in 3D space) - Links: 1 GB/s per direction - All-reduce latency: ~1 ms (synchronize across 512 TPUs in full Pod) NVIDIA H100 (multi-GPU): - Single GPU: Local Tensor Cores + NVLinks (900 GB/s between GPUs) - NVLink: 5 bidirectional links per GPU (18 total in DGX) - Effective: Crossbar-like behavior with 900 GB/s per link
