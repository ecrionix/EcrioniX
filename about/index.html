Who We Are

About EcrioniX

EcrioniX is a free learning platform built for electronics and semiconductor engineering students, professionals, and enthusiasts. Our goal is to make complex VLSI, RTL design, and digital electronics concepts approachable, accurate, and industry-relevant.

Our Mission

The semiconductor industry demands deep technical knowledge — yet most online resources are either too shallow for interview preparation or too dense for self-study. EcrioniX bridges this gap by providing structured, topic-by-topic guides written with the same depth expected in real ASIC and FPGA design roles.

Every article on EcrioniX is written to build genuine understanding — not just memorizable answers. We cover the "why" behind every concept, because engineers who understand fundamentals debug faster and design better.

Who This Is For

EcrioniX is built for engineering students preparing for VLSI interviews, working engineers refreshing their fundamentals, and anyone entering the chip design industry. Whether you are learning setup and hold time for the first time or reviewing SDC constraints before a project, you will find content here that meets you at your level.

All content is free. No signup required. No paywalls.

Our Values

What We Stand For

Every decision about how we write and build EcrioniX comes back to these principles.

01

Technical Accuracy

Every concept is written to be correct at the industry level — not simplified to the point of being misleading. If a detail matters in a real design review, it matters here.

02

Always Free

All content on EcrioniX is free to access. No subscriptions, no locked articles, no email walls. Learning should not have a paywall.

03

Structured Learning

Topics are organized into learning paths so you build knowledge progressively — foundational concepts before advanced ones, always in the right order.

04

Interactive First

Wherever possible, we include interactive simulators, labs, and visualizations — because seeing timing waveforms move is worth more than reading about them.

05

No Clutter

Clean design, fast loading, no pop-ups, no intrusive ads. We want nothing to get between you and the content you came for.

06

Continuously Updated

The semiconductor industry evolves. New topics, corrected explanations, and updated content are added regularly as EcrioniX grows.
