Metastability in RTL Design

Understanding one of the most critical challenges in Clock Domain Crossing

What is Metastability?

Metastability is a phenomenon where a flip-flop enters an unstable state when its setup or hold time is violated. The output can stay in an intermediate voltage level for an unpredictable amount of time before resolving to 0 or 1.
