Frequently Asked Questions

Are these PDF tools free to use?

Yes, completely free with no file-size limits and no watermarks on output.

Are my files uploaded to a server?

No. All processing is done in JavaScript inside your browser. Your files never leave your device.

How many files can I merge at once?

There is no artificial limit — only your device's available RAM. Most modern laptops handle dozens of PDFs fine.

Why is compression limited?

Browser-side compression removes metadata and re-serialises the PDF structure. Aggressive image resampling requires server-side tools. For heavy compression, the tool still saves you the roundtrip to a server.
